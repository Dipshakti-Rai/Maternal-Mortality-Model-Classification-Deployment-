Maternal Mortality Model Specification and News

Age Group
- Age group of patient.Range (10-15):0,(15-20):1,(20-25):2,(25-30):3,(30-35):4,(35-40):5,(40-45):5,(45-50):6,(50-55):7,(55-60):8,(60-65):9,(65-70):10,(70-):11. i.e for age of 27 pick 3 as in input feature.
Systolic Blood Pressure
- Measured blood pressure of patient. i.e blood pressure value 120/80 mmHg use 120 as an input feature.
Diastolic Blood Pressure
- Measured blood pressure of patient. i.e blood pressure value 120/80 mmHg use 80 as an diastolic blood pressure input.
Blood Sugar
- Blood sugar level of patient. When it was measured 140 mg/dL (7.8 mmol/L) use 7.8 as an input.
Body Temperature
- Like body temperature of 98.6°F (37°C) use 98.6 as body temeprature input.
Heart Rate
- Measured heart rate of patient when patent have heart rate of 60 beats per minute use 60 as an input feature.
